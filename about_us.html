About Us

Vipax Global Company LTD. Company is aimed to operate by finding solutions to major problems facing the business industries. Our scope of operation extends over to the foreign countries and we endeavor to increase the reach of our target market, explore in our field of specialization and maintain the best standards of operation for the company even as we provide technological solutions.

The company also stands as an incubation hub and job avenue that brings together IT specialists to collaborate and provide solutions while pursuing their career goals. We aim at bringing on board fresh graduates from the IT field as National Service Personnel and other upcoming computer programmers to train, practice and improve upon their skills and create employment for them as well. We believe in creating a reliable platform for developers to build projects, get employed and provide solutions to technological problems facing Churches, Businesses and other organizations.
